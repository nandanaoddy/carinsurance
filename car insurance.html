Car Insurance

What is Car Insurance?

Car insurance provides financial protection against physical damage or bodily injury resulting from traffic collisions, and against liability that could also arise from incidents in a vehicle.

Why Do You Need Car Insurance?

Car insurance is essential because it covers the expenses of repairs and medical bills in case of accidents. It also protects you from financial liability if you're responsible for an accident. Car insurance provides peace of mind knowing that you're financially secure in case of unexpected incidents on the road.

Types of Car Insurance Coverage:

1. Liability Coverage: This covers the costs related to injuries and property damage to others when you're at fault in an accident.

2. Collision Coverage: This helps pay for damage to your vehicle if you're involved in an accident with another vehicle.

3. Comprehensive Coverage: This covers damage to your car from events like theft, fire, or natural disasters.

4. Personal Injury Protection: This helps cover medical expenses for you and your passengers in case of an accident.

5. Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist Coverage: This protects you if you're involved in an accident with a driver who doesn't have insurance or doesn't have enough insurance to cover the damage.
